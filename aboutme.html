About Me

My Story

Hi, I’m Jenni and I'm a UX Designer. My past experience ranges from a client facing role in digital advertising, to more recently where I worked in merchandising at Wayfair. In each of my roles, I started to notice a pattern in the type of work that was given to me. Whenever there was a presentation or materials that needed to be created, those projects were always handed off to me. The context varied from sales decks to a department wide training. I’ve always had a knack for explaining information to people in a sequence that is easy to understand.

Before I made my career change, I thought about what job would utilize this. The more I learned about UX I discovered that my skills could be used to understand people and create intuitive products for them.
